What is LEAP?

The Leadership Education Accelerated Program (LEAP) is a 3-day, interactive workshop hosted by Global App Initiative that aims to help students gain valuable coding and leadership skills. LEAP participants will dive into topics ranging from Android application development, to design, to leadership skills. We will select 30 qualified participants based on their passion for learning and leading, and their goals for using their skills after the program concludes. Participants will have the opportunity to receive training from industry professionals and network with them. At the end of the program, participants will receive a certificate of completion. If interested in cultivating technical and leadership skills, apply using the form below.
