We are community members

Our ambition is to use the skills we learn to help our community. We partner with nonprofit organization to bridge the gap between technology and community service. Our partners' causes cover an array of social and economic problems including education, health care, and homelessness.

See the nonprofits we've helped

We are diverse

Students from several different schools in Boston University are a part of Global App Initiative's diverse community.Students' majors range from neuroscience to graphic design, and much more!

Just take a look at our team

We are learners

We encourage our members to continually grow in skill whether they are a veteran of app development or just now learning the ropes. Each week, students have the opporunity to constantly pick up new skills by attending office hours, workshops, and team meetings.

View tutorials and educational resources

We are leaders

Not only do members learn how to code and design apps, they learn what it takes to be a leader in GAI's administrative board. Our Marketing, Operations, Business Development, Education, and Client Relations committees enable motivated students to guide their peers to succeed.

Design. Develop. Debug.

With our organization a student will learn how to analyze a client's needs, generate innovative ideas with a team, create a wireframe design, establish two-way communication with nonprofits, program a mobile app using object-oriented techniques, and become leaders. By working with us, our experience becomes your experience and leaves you with a very unique set of skills that is otherwise difficult to obtain in classes.

Interested in being involved?

We'd love to have you! Regardless of background, experience, and knowledge, you are joining a community that is here to learn together.
