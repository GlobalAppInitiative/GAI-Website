Our Mission

Global App Initiative integrates humanity and technology to create a program that trains students to become mobile-app developers for nonprofits. Never losing sight of the bigger picture, we recruit students of all fashions and interests to work towards building a more economically, socially, and environmentally sound world.

Our History

August 2012

Clean App Your Neighborhood

Kern Family Foundation sponsored a “Clean App Your Neighborhood” workshop where 5 teams of 6 students built an app for a different nonprofit over 5 days.

November 2012

Apps For Humanity

Global App Initiative (initially named Apps For Humanity) was established as a BU student organization with 40 active members and four non-profit teams.

April 2014

Global Appathon

Global App Initiative hosts its first Global Appathon where more than 80 attendees created mobile apps.

May 2014

ESA Awards

Excellence in Student Activities awarded Global App Initiative with excellence in individual programming and best in service & justice.
